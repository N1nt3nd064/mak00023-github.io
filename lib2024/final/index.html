About

We started living there just as my sister was born, and lived in a small house before moving to a duplex in a suburb in order to be closer to my elementry school, which I attended from when it first opened, to when I graduated 10 years later. There were a lot of fun memories of my time living there, such as the various visits to West Edmonton Mall, and the time we spent with the friends we met over the years.
